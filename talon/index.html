Questions

Good to know.

Where does my bill data live? ▼
Entirely in your own Google Drive. Talon is a Google Sheet with built-in FalqenView automation that runs inside your Google account. Your finances are never transmitted to FalqenView or any third party.
Is this a subscription? ▼
No. Talon is a one-time purchase, the same as VuePoint. You download your copy and it's yours to use indefinitely — no recurring charges.
How many bills can I track? ▼
Unlimited. Add as many bills as you need — automatic or manual, one-time or recurring. Talon sorts and tracks them all by what's due next.
Does it sync with my calendar? ▼
Yes. Talon can push bill due dates to your Google Calendar so reminders show up alongside the rest of your schedule. It's optional and runs entirely inside your own Google account.
What happens to my data if I reset the sheet? ▼
Your bill history and settings are preserved in hidden backup sheets that survive a factory reset, and you can back up and restore your full data in a click.
