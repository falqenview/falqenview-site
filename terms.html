2. Product Description

FalqenView offers five categories of product. The first is paid digital tools delivered as Google Sheets templates with bound automation, including FalqenView VuePoint and FalqenView Talon (with FalqenView Updraft and FalqenView Apex planned). The second is Swoop, a paid browser extension ("Swoop Pro") that exports AI chat conversations from supported platforms to JSON or Markdown, with a free tier covering select platforms and a one-time paid unlock for additional platforms. The third is Swoop Recall, a browser-based web app that imports and archives the conversations you export with Swoop and makes them searchable, offered as a free tier (limited to a set number of conversations), a one-time paid "Pro" tier (unlimited local archive), and an optional yearly "Cloud" subscription tier (adding encrypted off-device backup). The fourth is Scrubr, a browser-based web app that inspects, redacts, and annotates hidden metadata in images and PDFs, offered as a free tier (Inspect and basic Annotate) and a one-time paid "Pro" tier (full Redact and Annotate). The fifth is free browser-based tools and apps, including Quba (a free Islamic prayer companion) and a set of free web utilities hosted at tools.falqenview.com (AI Prompt Architect, the Dev & Data Toolkit, the Writer's Toolkit, and the Generator).

For the paid Google Sheets products: upon purchase, you receive a link to create your own private copy of the Google Sheet. All automation, formatting, and features are included in that copy. A free Google account is required.

The free browser tools run entirely in your own browser. They require no account and no purchase, and nothing you enter into them is transmitted to or stored by FalqenView.
