Frequently asked questions

"Authorization Required" — what do I do? ▼
This is normal. When you open FalqenView for the first time and click any menu item, Google asks for permission before running any script. Click Review Permissions, sign in with your Google account, and approve. You'll only see this once. The script runs entirely within your own Google account and does not access any external data or other files.

If you see a "Google hasn't verified this app" warning on the next screen — click Advanced → Go to FalqenView (unsafe). This is expected for independently published tools and is safe to proceed.
Why isn't my ticker auto-filling? ▼
Make sure you're typing the ticker — not pasting. The auto-fill only triggers when you type directly into the Ticker cell on the Portfolio tab. If the ticker isn't in our database, the name and sector won't fill in but the price formula will still be set. You can manually type the position name and sector in those cells.
Prices aren't updating — how do I refresh? ▼
Go to FalqenView menu → Refresh Prices. This forces Google to recalculate all live prices. Note that Google Finance data is delayed 15–20 minutes by Google — this is a platform limitation, not a FalqenView issue. Prices update automatically when the sheet is open.
How do I claim upgrade pricing? ▼
Visit our support page and fill out the form with your original Gumroad order number. We'll send you a one-time coupon code as soon as possible. Coupons expire in 30 days and cannot be stacked with other offers.
What is Talon? ▼
Talon is a FalqenView product — a Google Sheets tracker built to manage your bills, due dates, categories, and cash obligations. It runs entirely in your own Google Drive and is sold as a one-time purchase, the same as VuePoint.
How do I set up Talon? ▼
Make your copy from the link in your purchase email (or the start-here page), then open the 🦅 FalqenView menu and authorize once — exactly like VuePoint. Your bills live only in your own Google Drive.
Can I add more than 5 positions per account? ▼
VuePoint supports up to 5 positions per account across up to 5 accounts — 25 positions in total. The structure is fixed; rows outside the defined account bands aren't tracked by the calculations. If you need more positions per account than the current version supports, submit a feature request through the form above — that's exactly the kind of feedback that shapes future releases.
My health score shows "—" — why? ▼
The health score requires at least one position with Shares, Total Paid, and Target % filled in. Once you add your first position on the Portfolio tab, the score will calculate automatically on the Summary tab.
Is my data private? ▼
Yes — completely. FalqenView lives entirely in your own Google account. We have no access to your data, no server connection, and nothing is transmitted outside your Google Drive. Your portfolio data is as private as your Google account.

That said, we stand behind what we've built. If you run into a technical issue, we'll work with you to resolve it. Reach us through the form on this page or at hello@falqenview.com — we'll get back to you as soon as humanly possible.
What is Swoop? ▼
Swoop is a browser extension for Chrome and Edge that exports your AI chat history — from Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Grok, and Copilot — as a clean, structured file. The primary use case is context-window management: long AI conversations consume tokens fast, and when a session gets too large, quality drops. Swoop lets you export, compress, and hand off your conversation history so you can start a fresh session without losing context. The free tier covers ChatGPT, Gemini, and Copilot. Swoop Pro (one-time purchase) unlocks Claude and Grok exports.
How do I activate Swoop Pro? ▼
After purchasing, open the Swoop extension in your browser and click Activate Pro. Enter the license key from your Gumroad receipt and click Verify. Activation is device-bound and verified once — after that it works offline. If you need to transfer your license to a new device, contact us through the form above.
My Swoop license key isn't being accepted — what do I do? ▼
First, make sure you're copying the key exactly as it appears in your Gumroad receipt — no extra spaces. If the error persists, check that you're connected to the internet during activation (it makes one verification call). Still stuck? Use the form above, select License / activation issue, and include your Gumroad order number — we'll sort it out.
