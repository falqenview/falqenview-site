The not-so-boring story

Built to put you back in control of your money.

FalqenView didn't start with a pitch deck. It started with a long string of frustrations — and a decision to stop linking and start building.

Like most self-directed investors, I tried what everyone recommends — portfolio apps, budgeting dashboards, account aggregators. The pitch is always the same: link your accounts and get the full picture. Simple.

Except it never is. The linkages break. Repeatedly. You reconnect, re-authorize, re-trust — over and over. Half your accounts aren't even supported, so the "complete" picture is never actually complete. And the whole time you're left wondering exactly who has eyes on your balances, your positions, your transaction history. The answers are never satisfying.

Bills management was no different. Every budgeting tool demanded access. Every app needed a connection. One broken link and the whole dashboard collapses — back to maintenance mode, back to re-authorizing services you were never fully comfortable trusting in the first place.

Eventually, enough was enough. So I stopped linking and started building.

A sheet that lives in your Google account — not ours, not a third party's. Your data doesn't travel anywhere. There's nothing to reconnect, nothing to re-authorize, and no server on the other end wondering what to do with your financial life.

VuePoint tracks what you invest. Talon tracks what you owe. Both do it without touching a system you don't control. No maintenance. No broken connections. No privacy guesswork. Just the full, clear picture — always in your hands.
