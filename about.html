Our story

Built to put you back in control of your money.

FalqenView started as a personal frustration. Managing multiple accounts — IRA, 401k, taxable, HSA — shouldn't require a Bloomberg terminal or a financial advisor with a logo.

So we built the tracker we wanted. No monthly fee. No data sharing. No fluff. Just a sheet that lives in your own Google account and gives you the clarity to invest like a grown-up.

Talon is that same instinct pointed at the other half of the problem — the bills. The falcon sees from above and grips below. Two sheets, one philosophy: own your numbers.
