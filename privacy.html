Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 25, 2026  ·  Last revised: June 19, 2026  ·  This policy may be updated at any time without prior notice. Continued use of the site or product constitutes acceptance of the current policy.
1. Privacy as a Foundation

Privacy is not a feature at FalqenView. It is the foundation. These tools were built the way I use tools: no one watching, no data leaving, no system sitting between you and your own information.

FalqenView does not collect, store, or process your personal data. There are no analytics tools, no advertising trackers, and no third-party data collection of any kind embedded in this website or in any FalqenView product.

When you submit a form on this site — including the contact form or any product waitlist — your name and email address are routed to our inbox to respond to your inquiry or to notify you when a product becomes available. That information is not stored beyond that purpose and is not shared with any third party.

2. Purchases

All purchase transactions are processed by Gumroad. FalqenView does not collect, see, or store your payment information. Any data collected during checkout — including your email address and payment details — is governed by Gumroad's Privacy Policy.

Your purchase email may be retained by FalqenView solely to verify buyer status when you request access to a product update.

3. Your Data
We never see your data. All information you enter into a FalqenView product — portfolio positions, bill amounts, account names, balances, or any other financial detail — lives exclusively where you put it: your own Google account. There is no FalqenView server receiving it, no database storing it, and no one with access to it. Not now, not ever.

FalqenView's free browser tools — including all utilities hosted at tools.falqenview.com — run entirely within your browser. Nothing you type or paste into them is transmitted to FalqenView or any third party. No account is required, and no data persists beyond your current session.

Quba, FalqenView's free Islamic prayer app, stores your preferences and prayer tracking locally on your device using your browser's built-in storage. That information never leaves your device and is never transmitted to or stored by FalqenView. When you use the mosque finder feature, your approximate location is sent through FalqenView's servers to Google's Places API solely to return nearby mosque results. FalqenView does not log or store your location. That query is governed by Google's privacy policy.

FalqenView holds no copies of your financial or personal data. There is no FalqenView database to compromise. Any security exposure related to your data would exist entirely within systems you already own and control — your Google account or your device.

No mothership. No back door. No exceptions.
3a. Third-Party Services & Infrastructure

FalqenView uses a small number of third-party services to operate this site and its products. None of these services receive your financial data, portfolio information, or personal files. FalqenView does not use advertising networks, behavioral tracking platforms, or analytics services of any kind.

  • Cloudflare — FalqenView's website and tools are served through Cloudflare's infrastructure. Cloudflare may process standard request metadata, including IP addresses, as part of its network operations. This is governed by Cloudflare's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — Typography on this site is loaded from Google's font servers. Google may log font request metadata including IP addresses. This is governed by Google's privacy policy.
  • Gumroad — All purchases are processed by Gumroad. See §2 above for details.
  • Google Places API — Used by Quba's mosque finder to return location-based results. See §3 above for details.
  • YouTube — FalqenView's videos page references content hosted on YouTube. Any interaction with YouTube content is subject to YouTube's privacy policy.

If FalqenView adds a new third-party service that materially changes how data is handled, this section will be updated to reflect it.

4. Why This Matters

Most software is designed to learn about you. FalqenView is designed to work for you. The distinction is intentional.

I chose to build products where the only person with access to your financial and personal data is you. Not because privacy is good marketing. Because handing someone else visibility into your accounts — even passively, even "anonymized" — is a trade you should never have to make just to use a decent tool.

FalqenView keeps its focus on the work. No founder profile. No personal brand attached to the products. The tools stand on their own.

5. Contact

Questions about this policy may be directed to us via the support page at falqenview.com/support.
