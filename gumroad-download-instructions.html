Setup Instructions
1

Click the download link & make your copy

Click the Download FalqenView VuePoint button above. You'll be taken to Google Sheets where you'll be prompted to make a copy. Click Make a copy — this saves your personal copy directly to your Google Drive. You own it completely; nothing is shared back.

ℹ️ You must be signed into a Google account to complete this step. If you're not signed in, Google will prompt you to do so first.
2

Open the FalqenView menu to authorize

Once your copy opens, look for the 🦅 FalqenView menu in the Google Sheets top menu bar. Click it, then click About FalqenView. This triggers the one-time authorization prompt.

A popup will appear saying "Authorization required — A script attached to this document needs your permission to run." Click OK to proceed.

Screenshot 1 — Authorization required dialog
3

Choose your Google account

Google will ask which account to authorize with. Select the Google account you used to make your copy.

4

"Google hasn't verified this app" — this is expected

You'll see a screen with a red warning triangle that reads "Google hasn't verified this app." This is a standard Google notice that appears for all independent Apps Script products — it does not mean the product is unsafe.

Screenshot 2 — Standard Google verification notice (expected)
🛡️
Why does this appear?
Google displays this warning for any script not enrolled in their formal OAuth verification program — a process designed for large commercial apps. Independent tools built on Google Sheets trigger this notice by default, regardless of how safe or contained the product is.

FalqenView VuePoint does not access, read, or transmit any data outside your own Google account. The product runs entirely within your personal copy of the spreadsheet. No data leaves your Drive. No external servers are involved.

Click Advanced at the bottom left of the screen to expand the options.

Screenshot 3 — Click "Advanced" to continue

Then click "Go to FalqenView - VuePoint v1.3.3 (unsafe)" at the bottom of the expanded section. The word "unsafe" is Google's default label for unverified scripts — it does not reflect the actual safety of this product.

5

Grant permissions & click Continue

The final screen lists what FalqenView VuePoint is requesting access to. You'll see two items:

  • See, edit, create, and delete all your Google Sheets spreadsheets
  • Display and run third-party web content in prompts and sidebars

Both permissions are required for the FalqenView menu, formula refresh, and the About modal to function. Make sure both are checked, then click Continue.

Screenshot 4 — Select all permissions and click Continue
ℹ️ These permissions apply only to your personal copy of FalqenView VuePoint inside your own Google Drive. The script cannot read, access, or modify any other files in your account.

🔒   What FalqenView does and does not do

  • Runs 100% within your personal Google Drive — no external servers, no outside access
  • Does not collect, store, or transmit any of your financial data
  • Does not read any Google Sheets files outside your own copy
  • Does not send emails, access Gmail, or interact with any other Google service
  • All calculations happen locally inside your spreadsheet
  • Your portfolio data never leaves your Google account
6

Authorization complete — you're ready

Once you click Continue, the FalqenView About modal will appear inside your spreadsheet. When you see it, authorization is complete and the product is fully activated.

✅ This one-time authorization is all that's needed. You'll never be prompted again for this copy of FalqenView VuePoint.
