Private by architecture

Your data has nowhere else to go.

Not private by policy — private by design. There is no FalqenView server to send anything to, no account to create, no database holding a copy. Everything runs where you already are: your device, your browser, your Drive.

No accounts No servers Nothing phones home

Designed, not templated

Software that feels finished.

Clean dashboards, smooth interactions, and interfaces that were designed — never assembled. Most tools install as their own Mobile Dashboard (WebApp), with its own icon and window. Nothing here looks like a form someone forgot to style.

Open it. Use it. That's it.

No ads, no nags, no pop-ups asking how it's going. No dark patterns steering you anywhere. Every tool does one thing, does it well, and then gets out of your way — the way software used to work.
