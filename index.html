Why it's different

Two things every other
money app gets wrong.

Your financial data should be yours alone — and the tool you keep it in should feel like a joy to use. FalqenView is built on both.

Your data, only yours. Full stop.

It lives in your Google account — and never leaves it.

No app. No account. No server. Just a Google Sheet sitting in your Drive — yours, completely. There's no FalqenView login, no database, no backend — and nothing that phones home. We cannot see your numbers, because they never reach us. Your portfolio and your bills stay in your account alone. Not ours.

That makes it exactly as private and secure as your Google account itself. With ordinary finance apps, you can never really know what's happening to your data behind the scenes. Here there is no behind the scenes — it's your sheet, doing only what you can see it do.
