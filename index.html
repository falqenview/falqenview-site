Why it's different

Two things every other
money app gets wrong.

Your financial data should be yours alone — and the tool you keep it in should feel like a joy to use. FalqenView is built on both.

Your data, only yours

It lives in your Google account — and never leaves it.

FalqenView is a Google Sheet you own, sitting in your Drive. There's no FalqenView account, no server, no database, no login — and nothing that phones home. We literally cannot see your numbers, because they never reach us.

That makes it exactly as private and secure as your Google account itself. With ordinary finance apps, you can never really know what's happening to your data behind the scenes. Here there is no behind the scenes — it's your sheet, doing only what you can see it do.

No accounts No servers No telemetry Zero reach-back

Designed like a real product

It will not feel like a spreadsheet.

This is not your ordinary, formula-cluttered, Excel-style Google Sheet. Every screen is designed — clean dashboards, live health scores, drift and signal alerts, even a real mobile WebApp. The grid disappears; what's left feels like software you'd happily pay monthly for.

You get the ownership and durability of a sheet you control, with the polish of a finished app. Most people don't believe it's built on Google Sheets until they open the formulas.

Common questions

Before you buy

If you've read this far, you have questions. Probably these.

Do I need Excel or Microsoft 365?+

Nope. FalqenView lives in Google Sheets. If you have a free Google account, you have everything you need — no Excel, no Microsoft 365, no separate app to install.

What if I have more than 5 accounts?+

VuePoint covers 5 accounts × 5 positions out of the box. Updraft removes the 5 positions per-account cap when it ships.

How private is my portfolio data?+

Completely. The sheet lives in your Google Drive. We don't have a server, an API, or a way to see your data. Nothing is transmitted outside your Google account.

Will it work on my phone?+

The sheet opens in the Google Sheets mobile app on iOS and Android — view-only is comfortable, edits are workable. A dedicated mobile dashboard (live values, alerts, account breakdowns) ships with Apex.

Is this a subscription? Will it auto-renew?+

No. One charge. No renewal. No "free trial that auto-charges in 14 days." Lifetime use, and all updates within your tier are included forever.

Can I upgrade to Updraft or Apex later?+

Yes — at upgrade pricing. Reach out with your original order number and we'll set you up. Start with VuePoint now and your upgrade path opens the moment Updraft and Apex ship.

What's Talon — and is it the same as VuePoint?+

Talon is the bills-and-cash half of FalqenView. VuePoint tracks what you invest; Talon manages what you owe — bills, due dates, categories, autopay coverage, and a bills health score. Same idea (a Google Sheet you own, one-time purchase), different job. Buy either, or both.

Is Talon included with Apex?+

Yes. Talon ships free with Apex — the top investing tier — so the whole arsenal sits in one place. On its own, Talon is $14, once. (Updraft does not include Talon.)
