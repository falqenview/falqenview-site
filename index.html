Common questions

Before you buy

If you've read this far, you have questions. Probably these.

Do I need Excel or Microsoft 365?+

Nope. FalqenView lives in Google Sheets. If you have a free Google account, you have everything you need — no Excel, no Microsoft 365, no separate app to install.

What if I have more than 5 accounts?+

VuePoint covers 5 accounts × 5 positions out of the box. Updraft removes the 5 positions per-account cap when it ships.

How private is my portfolio data?+

Completely. The sheet lives in your Google Drive. We don't have a server, an API, or a way to see your data. Nothing is transmitted outside your Google account.

Will it work on my phone?+

The sheet opens in the Google Sheets mobile app on iOS and Android — view-only is comfortable, edits are workable. A dedicated mobile dashboard (live values, alerts, account breakdowns) ships with Apex.

Is this a subscription? Will it auto-renew?+

No. One charge. No renewal. No "free trial that auto-charges in 14 days." Lifetime use, and all updates within your tier are included forever.

Can I upgrade to Updraft or Apex later?+

Yes — at upgrade pricing. Reach out with your original order number and we'll set you up. Joining the waitlist on this page also locks in launch pricing.
