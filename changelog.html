VuePoint
v1.3 Latest
Multi-account, dark theme & deep refinement
  • change: multi-account restructure — track several accounts with per-account subtotals and a portfolio grand total
  • change: full dark-theme redesign, refined end-to-end — signal and tax-lot pills, ticker badges, and account bands all tuned for clearer reading
  • add: monitoring suite — cash drag, sector concentration, rebalance hints, and top movers surfaced across your accounts
  • add: a Watchlist to track tickers you're weighing before they go into the portfolio
  • add: completeness cues and inline drift bars — read the portfolio at a glance without reading the numbers
  • add: first-run setup wizard — guides account names and your drift threshold on first open; re-runnable anytime
  • fix: calculations hardened across the board — short holds, missing dates, and incomplete rows now render cleanly instead of throwing errors
v1.2
Signals, scoring & full visual rebuild
  • change: complete visual rebuild — dark theme, brand palette, and refined typography across every tab
  • add: KPI banner — total value, cost basis, and gains pinned to the top of your portfolio at all times
  • add: signal column — at-a-glance, color-coded action signals based on your drift and gain state
  • add: annualized return per position, with color-coded drift and signal cues for instant scanning
  • add: a Settings tab to configure your thresholds and account names in one place
  • change: broader built-in ticker coverage, clearer unknown-symbol alerts, and a cleaner gridline-free reading surface
v1.0
Launch — the foundation
  • init: multi-account portfolio tracker — several accounts with subtotals and a portfolio grand total
  • add: ticker auto-fill — enter a symbol and position details populate automatically
  • add: gain/loss tracking with automatic long-term eligibility dates
  • add: target allocations, drift alerts, and an A–F portfolio health score
  • add: a built-in sample portfolio to learn from before entering your own holdings
Talon Bills & Cash
v1.3 Latest
A sharper look, smoother sidebar & steadier reliability
  • change: a refreshed, brand-aligned look across the sheet — clearer colors and a cleaner layout that's easier to read at a glance
  • add: payment method styling — bank, card, cash, and mobile pay each show with their own color and icon so you can spot them instantly
  • add: an amount view that highlights your bigger bills at a glance, so cost weight reads without scanning every number
  • change: a roomier, streamlined sidebar — more space for your bills and less visual clutter
  • change: smoother, faster category and payment-method selection when adding or editing a bill
  • add: a daily email digest and calendar reminders so nothing slips past due
  • change: a fairer bills health grade that reflects setups of any size
  • add: one-click backup and restore — save a private snapshot of all your bills and bring them back anytime, even after a full reset
  • add: quick filters — jump straight to what's overdue, due soon, manual, or on autopay with a single tap
  • add: bill history — see how a bill has changed over time, right from its edit screen
  • add: mark paid with instant undo — clear a bill in one tap, and undo it just as fast if you change your mind
  • add: automatic tidy-up — one-time bills move out of your way once paid, so your active list stays clean
  • change: a cleaner fresh start — resetting now gives you a polished, ready-to-use sheet instead of a blank one
  • fix: a more dependable startup — your bills stay exactly as you left them
  • fix: a range of polish and stability improvements across the sheet and sidebar
v1.2
Categories, totals & health score refinement
  • add: category breakdown — housing, utilities, subscriptions, and more grouped with per-category totals surfaced in the sheet
  • add: monthly and annual bill totals — see your full payment load at a glance without touching a formula
  • change: health score recalibrated — autopay coverage and frequency-mix weighted more precisely for a fairer A–F grade
  • change: FalqenView icon added to the sidebar header — brand presence inside the panel, consistent with VuePoint
v1.1
Sidebar refinement & bill annotations
  • add: notes field — attach a short annotation to any bill, visible at a glance in the sidebar
  • change: sidebar layout expanded and re-spaced for cleaner field alignment and easier editing
  • add: animated brand header in the sidebar with version tag — polish and product identity in one strip
  • fix: date calculations hardened — bills at month-end and across calendar transitions now calculate correctly
v1.0
Launch — bills & cash, in your grip
  • init: a simple but complete bills manager — every bill in one Google Sheet you own
  • add: auto & manual bill tracking, with a clear status for what still needs your hand
  • add: a due-date calendar with reminders so nothing slips past due
  • add: categories — group housing, utilities, and subscriptions, and see where the money goes
  • add: real-time bills health score (A–F) from autopay coverage and due-soon vs. past-due
  • add: monthly & annual totals, plus a built-in sample to learn from before your own bills go in
