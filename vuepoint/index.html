Questions

Good to know.

Where does my portfolio data live? ▼
Entirely in your own Google Drive. VuePoint is a Google Sheet with built-in FalqenView automation that runs inside your Google account. Your holdings are never transmitted to FalqenView or any third party.
Is this a subscription? ▼
No. VuePoint is a one-time purchase. You download your copy, and it's yours to use indefinitely — no recurring charges, no renewals.
How many positions can I track? ▼
VuePoint tracks up to 5 positions across each of up to 5 accounts — 25 positions in total — with dedicated calculations for each. If you need more, send a feature request through the support form; that feedback shapes future releases.
Where do the prices come from? ▼
Live prices come through Google Finance inside your sheet. Note that Google Finance data is delayed roughly 15–20 minutes — a platform limitation of Google's, not VuePoint.
Do I need to be an expert to use it? ▼
No. You type tickers and shares; VuePoint handles the math. The signals and health score are designed to make sophisticated portfolio analysis readable at a glance, whatever your experience level.
