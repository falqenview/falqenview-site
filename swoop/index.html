Questions

Good to know.

Which browsers does Swoop support? ▼
Swoop runs on Chrome, Edge, and Firefox. It's the same package across all three — install it from the store listing and the floating export button appears right in your supported AI chats.
What's free and what needs Pro? ▼
ChatGPT, Gemini, and Copilot are free, with no account or key required. Claude and Grok unlock with Swoop Pro — a one-time purchase whose single license key enables both.
Is Swoop Pro a subscription? ▼
No. Swoop Pro is a one-time purchase. One license key unlocks both Pro platforms with no subscription and no renewal.
Does Swoop send my conversations anywhere? ▼
No. Swoop reads the conversation on the page you're viewing and writes it to a file locally. There's no account, no API key, and no server — nothing leaves your browser.
How does Swoop work with Swoop Recall? ▼
Swoop is how you get conversations out of each AI platform; Swoop Recall is where you archive and search them. Export with Swoop, import into Swoop Recall, and you've got a searchable record of everything you've discussed.
